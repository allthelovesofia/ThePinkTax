ALL ABOUT THE PINK TAX

        The term “Pink Tax” is coined by activists from the misogynistic and gender-biased society we are living in. There is a big difference when it comes to men’s necessities and women’s necessities especially in variety and prices. This is a form of marketing that is totally unnecessary and unorthodox to the women’s community.
This site is made for the purpose of raising awareness to everybody’s well-being that there is an evident gender inequality, especially towards women, happening in today’s society. This site is all about women empowerment without the need to bring down other genders and just by showcasing the capacity and abilities of women.
The pages are inclusive of the events showcasing women empowerment, gender equalities, and protests against the “Pink Tax”. Slogans and posters are also posted to give knowledge about what to do and how to react to certain issues regarding the gender-inequality related stuff towards women. Also to be shown are the women of all time who have been a big part and benefactors to the development of women’s rights from its darkest days up until today.
The site also seeks to give information while still being able to give entertainment to its readers and viewers. A well-designed and properly-built site would acquire attention from a lot of people regardless of who they are. This is already a huge step towards opening people’s minds and raising awareness. The ambiance of the site is a place where certain ideas and question are to be raised but no unaccepting behavior is to be tolerated for an open-minded society is what the goal of this site is in the first place.
